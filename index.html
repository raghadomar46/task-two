Qalqilia

Qalqilya is a city located in the northwestern part of the West Bank in Palestine, near the Green Line that separates it from the lands occupied in 1948. Qalqilya is known for its rich history and lush natural landscape, with numerous orchards of citrus fruits and olives covering its lands. The city is relatively small but is notable for its agricultural and commercial significance. It also has tourist attractions, such as the famous zoo, which is the only one in the West Bank and attracts many local and international visitors.
